How it Works

Welcome to Foodiliciousbite Guide

For Buyers:

  • On the Home Page You Choose the city your currently want to order in, Optionally you can also choose the dish name as well
  • You will be guided to Product page, where all your selected dishes will be viewed that are available in your city
  • You can view any of the dish by clicking on the title of the dish
  • Using the contact provided by the seller you can call them and order the dish
  • Furthermore all the details like delivery time and ingredients are to be discussed between you and the seller.
  • Foddiliciousbite doesnot involve in the delivery of the products

For Sellers:

  • On the Home Page You can click on the Sell My Dish in the Navigation bar
  • You will be guided to Add Product page
  • On the Product Page provide the tilte of the Dish(Compulsory)
  • Provide at least Two real photos of your dish. (Compulsory)
  • Select the serving of your dish, By default it is one but you can change it accordingly
  • Provide the details of your dish. Include everything that a user could possibly search for. In case of more than one dishes mention the dish name. Furthermore also provide the expected delivery time (Compulsory)
  • Also provide the expected delivery time, like 1 hour, 2 hours. And Delivery Charges in Areas close or further from your Area
  • Next, Provide the Price of the dish (Compulsory)
  • Select Area where you live in, So that user are aware of the expected delivery time (Only Compulsory if Delivery In Some Areas of City or Closer ones)
  • Provide City Name (Compulsory)
  • Choose a delivery scope , In case you only provide delivery in areas closer to your area
  • Note: Google images should not be added and in case of report the product will be removed
